After much demand, the Digital Data Conference is back! **Digital Data 2026: Enabling Science Through Integrated Biodiversity Data will be held as a virtual event on June 2-4, 2026.**

The conference will bring together the community to explore how integrated biodiversity data enable research, innovation, and impact across disciplines. We are excited to continue the conversation and build momentum around data-driven biodiversity science.

**More information, including the program, themes, and speakers, are available on the [event page](https://events.zoom.us/ev/Aifs2F4FYj-AMdSXZZ1uRq47AgWVjZGdR040MyHfv_gDdYVQc5Z_~AgMNQwYdwFOW4vc08eAy6pa4Xhi5Qg0tRrw3tezXQ70bqsGV-wZ6OHsUfA).**

Please note that registration is required to submit an abstract. Abstract submission links will be included in your ticket confirmation email.

**Key Dates:**
- April 1st: Abstract submissions & registration opens
- May 1st: Abstract submission deadline
- May 15th: Final agenda published
- June 2-4: Conference
