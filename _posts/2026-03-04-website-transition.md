iDigBio has transitioned its web presence to this [Hosted Portal](./) powered by [GBIF](https://www.gbif.org/). Our legacy website and wiki at [idigbio.org](https://www.idigbio.org) will redirect to this hoted portal for the forseeable future.

Note that access to the legacy [iDigBio data portal](https://portal.idigbio.org/) and the active [Symbiota network portals](https://symbiota.org/symbiota-portals/) are ***not affected*** by this transition.
