iDigBio has transitioned its website and wiki to this Hosted Portal powered by GBIF. Our legacy website, [www.idigbio.org](https://www.idigbio.org), will redirect here for the forseeable future. Note that access to the [iDigBio data portal](https://portal.idigbio.org/) or the [Symbiota network portals](https://symbiota.org/symbiota-portals/) are *not affected* by this transition.

The legacy iDigBio website and wiki have been archived at the Internet Archive's Wayback Machine and will be available in perpetuity: [web.archive.org/web/idigbio.org](https://web.archive.org/web/idigbio.org)
